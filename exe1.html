Introduction

The curriculum for the Bachelor Of Technology in Computer Science program at ABES Engineering College Ghaziabad is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science principles, programming languages, software development, and emerging technologies. The program aims to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the field of computer science and contribute to technological advancements.
